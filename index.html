Game Rules

Goal of the Game: Create a continuous circular railway line that reaches every place where the train can travel.

Terrain, bridges, and mountains must be navigated. The railway line cannot:

  • Cross itself
  • Pass through a lake
  • Pass through a cell more than once

Controls:

  • Left-click: Place a rail and change rail type
  • Right-click: Rotate rail
